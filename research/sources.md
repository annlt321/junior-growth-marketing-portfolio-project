- **Current Role:** Founder at Five Exit (Top community for B2B marketing professionals.)
- **Brief Annotation:**
Having managed marketing for high growth SaaS companies like Drift and Privy, Dave represents the founder led marketing perspective. Because he started as a B2B SaaS CMO and now operates a community for SaaS marketers, Dave is an exceptionally practical resource to reference when building an organic LinkedIn strategy for B2B SaaS [1](https://www.linkedin.com/in/davegerhardt).
