### #1 Religiously Commenting
Yes, commenting brings clients to others.
That's because their comments are packed with relevance.

The words 'quality' and 'value'are turning cliches. But one thing is for sure, Great Post A wesome Insights Let's Connect Superb! are not going to cut through either.

TIP:
Write comments that
• Add additional information
• Challenge the Thought of the Post or Comment
• Ask relevant or Thought Provoking Questions
• Show your appreciation and why

### #2 Getting Burned Out
This is the result of not knowing your endgame. Not knowing your purpose.
I hate to see people wasting time commenting to over 50 posts a day without being intentional about it.

TIP:
What's the purpose of your daily commenting routine? Write it down. Set the time. Start. Repeat.
Example:
**Goal:** Get at least 5 Profile Views Everday from Target Audience
**Task:** Leave relevant comments on posts from your target audience
**Do this for the next 3-6 months. Observe the results.**

### #4 Wasted Year
You don't have to wait for a year to realize that you aren't getting results.
You keep stepping on dog poop for the whole year and you're complaining that it's stinky today?
This might be your wake-up call.

TIP:
Complaining will not do anything good. Get into the root cause of your problem.
Zero Booked Calls? Why? No leads? Why? No Conversion? Why? Difficulty in Hiring? Why? Not getting anyjob opportunities? Why?
What 3 Steps can you take NOW to change the outcome?
Within 3-6 months, observe your results. Pivot.
