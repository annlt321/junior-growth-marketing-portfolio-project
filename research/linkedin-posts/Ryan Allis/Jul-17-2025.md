The Strategy: Build Your B2B Content Machine
1. Build an ABM lead list with tools like Instantly, Apollo, and ListKit
2. Send high-value content via a 10-email outbound sequence
3. Launch Matched Audience Ads on Meta, LinkedIn, Google, Adroll — to the same leads
4. Layer on retargeting, lookalike, search, and review site ads
5. Have an SDR follow up with engaged prospects

Results:
🔹 60,000 new leads reached/month
🔹 15,000 site visitors/month
🔹 1,000 MQLs/month
🔹 $3M ARR from $180k S&M spend
🔹 1M B2B impressions/month for $16.7k/month

Key Takeaway:
🔸 Don’t wait for leads to find you. Go find them.
🔸 Educate them with great content. Build trust. 
🔸 Then invite them to take action (trial, demo, consultation, etc.)
