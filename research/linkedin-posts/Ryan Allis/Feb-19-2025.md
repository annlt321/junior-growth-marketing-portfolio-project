The Modern B2B Content Strategy

Traditional SEO-driven inbound marketing is dying. AI-generated answers and shifts in search behavior have changed the game. Instead of relying on outdated tactics, we use a multi-channel content distribution strategy that combines:
- ABM list building
- Outbound & paid ads
- Weekly newsletters
- LinkedIn thought leadership

We don’t wait for traffic. We put our content directly in front of our ICP using tools like:
- Clay, Apollo, Instantly, ListKit (to build a highly targeted ABM lead list)
- AI-personalized outbound outreach (to reach SaaS CEOs & Founders)
- BeeHiiv newsletters + LinkedIn thought leadership ads (to nurture and educate)
- Retargeting ads on Meta, Google, LinkedIn, and Adroll (to stay top of mind)
