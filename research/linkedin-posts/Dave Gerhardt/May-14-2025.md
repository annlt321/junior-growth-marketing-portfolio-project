## Content

Teach them. The best B2B marketing strategy is to be the number one resource for your customers.

B2B often means "at work" so you're helping people do their jobs better.

Ideally your company was created not because the founder had some vision in the middle of the night and woke up and said "I'm going to get rich by starting a professional services or manufacturing business!" but the founder has some deep experience or pain in that industry and was led to start a company to solve that problem.

You can build a brand around helping your customers win by teaching them how to solve that problem too. Not specifically with your product of course, but generally - become the number one resource for them.

You don't need to out spend, out sell, or out sponsor the competitors in your industry -- but you should try to out teach them. Be the best teacher for your ideal customers, and the best marketing can come from there.

There’s also no better differentiator than showing you know how to solve your customers problems
